7 Signs Your Laptop Needs Repair — Don't Ignore These Warning Signs

Is your laptop acting up? These are the key signs that your laptop needs professional repair — and what you should do about it in Bhuj.

📅 February 23, 2026 ✍️ Hari Tech Solutions Editorial Team ⏱️ 5 min read 📁 Laptop Repair

Is Your Laptop Telling You Something?

Laptops are essential tools for work, education, and communication. But like all machines, they wear out over time. Many users in Bhuj continue using a laptop that is showing clear signs of failure—costing themselves more in data loss and productivity than the cost of a simple repair.

At Hari Tech Solutions in Bhuj, we see hundreds of laptops every month. Here are the 7 most common warning signs that your laptop needs professional repair—and what to do about it.

Sign 1: Laptop Running Very Slow

A slow laptop is one of the most common complaints we hear in Bhuj. If your laptop takes forever to boot, load apps, or respond to clicks, it could be due to a full hard drive, a failing HDD, too little RAM, or malware infection. A simple SSD upgrade at Hari Tech Solutions can make your old laptop feel brand new—often at a fraction of the cost of a new device.

Sign 2: Laptop Overheating

Does your laptop get extremely hot during use, or does the fan run loudly and constantly? Overheating is a serious sign. It can be caused by clogged air vents, a failing cooling fan, or dried-out thermal paste on the processor. Left untreated, overheating can permanently damage your motherboard. Our technicians in Bhuj clean internal components and replace thermal paste to bring temperatures back to normal.

Sign 3: Laptop Won't Turn On

If your laptop is completely dead—no lights, no screen, no sound—it could be a battery failure, charging port damage, or a motherboard fault. Don't panic. Bring it to Hari Tech Solutions in Bhuj for a free diagnosis. Many no-power laptops are fixed the same day.

Sign 4: Cracked or Flickering Screen

A cracked screen, persistent flickering, or discoloration are clear signs you need a screen replacement. We stock replacement screens for all major laptop brands in Bhuj—HP, Dell, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, and Apple MacBooks. Screen replacement is usually completed same-day at competitive prices.

Sign 5: Battery Draining Too Fast

If your laptop battery drains in under an hour or does not charge at all, the battery needs replacement. We replace laptop batteries for all brands in Bhuj. Always use quality replacement batteries—cheap third-party batteries can cause overheating or short-circuits.

Sign 6: Keyboard or Touchpad Not Working

Stuck keys, unresponsive touchpad, or keys typing the wrong characters are all signs of keyboard damage. This can happen due to liquid spillage, physical damage, or connector faults. We repair and replace keyboards for all laptop models in Bhuj quickly and affordably.

Sign 7: Frequent Crashes and Blue Screen Errors

If your laptop crashes frequently, shows blue screen errors (BSOD), or has programs freezing constantly, this could indicate failing RAM, a corrupt operating system, or a dying hard drive. Don't lose your data—bring your laptop to Hari Tech Solutions in Bhuj for a full hardware and software diagnosis.

Best CCTV Cameras for Shop Security in Bhuj — 2026 Guide

Thinking of installing CCTV for your shop in Bhuj? Here's everything you need to know about choosing the right security cameras for your business.

📅 February 23, 2026 ✍️ Hari Tech Solutions Editorial Team ⏱️ 6 min read 📁 CCTV & Security

Why Every Shop in Bhuj Needs CCTV in 2026

Shop theft, employee accountability, customer disputes, and insurance claims—these are everyday realities for business owners in Bhuj. A reliable CCTV system is the most cost-effective way to protect your investment, monitor your business remotely, and provide undeniable evidence when needed.

At Hari Tech Solutions, we have installed CCTV for hundreds of shops, showrooms, restaurants, and warehouses across Bhuj and Kutch. Here is our expert guide to choosing the best CCTV cameras for your shop in Bhuj.

What to Look for in a CCTV Camera for Your Shop

  • Resolution: Minimum 2MP (1080p) for clear face and number plate identification. 4MP or 4K cameras provide much sharper images for large shops.
  • Night Vision: Essential for shops in Bhuj. Look for cameras with at least 20–30 meters of IR night vision range.
  • Indoor vs Outdoor: Choose weatherproof (IP66-rated) cameras for exterior mounting. Standard dome cameras work indoors.
  • Wide Angle vs Varifocal: Wide-angle cameras (2.8mm lens) cover broad areas. Varifocal cameras let you zoom in for tighter coverage of entrances or cash counters.
  • Storage: 1TB HDD in a DVR/NVR typically stores 15–30 days of continuous footage for a 4-camera system at 1080p.

Top CCTV Camera Brands Available in Bhuj

Hikvision is the world's leading CCTV brand and our top recommendation for shops in Bhuj. Excellent build quality, wide range, and reliable after-sales support. Ideal for businesses that require high reliability.

CP Plus is an Indian brand with a strong presence in Bhuj and Kutch. Excellent value for money with good local support. Recommended for small to medium shops with standard security needs.

Securus is a budget-friendly option that performs well for basic monitoring needs. Good choice for very small shops or homes in Bhuj on a tight budget.

How Many Cameras Does Your Shop Need?

The number of cameras depends on the size and layout of your shop. As a general guide for shops in Bhuj:

  • Small shop (under 500 sq ft): 2 cameras — one at the entrance, one covering the billing/cash counter
  • Medium shop (500–2000 sq ft): 4 cameras — entrance, cash counter, storage area, and a wide-angle for the main floor
  • Large showroom or warehouse: 8–16 cameras with strategic placement across all zones

Our technicians in Bhuj do a free site survey before installation to recommend the optimal camera count and placement for complete coverage.
