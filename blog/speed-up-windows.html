Simple steps to make your computer feel like new again. No tech skills needed!

Is your PC slow?

Windows can get heavy over time. Here is how you fix it in 3 minutes:

🚀
1. Stop Auto-Start Apps Right-click your Taskbar -> Task Manager -> Startup. Disable anything you don't need immediately. This saves memory!
🧹
2. Storage Sense Go to Settings -> System -> Storage. Turn on 'Storage Sense'. It will automatically delete temporary junk files for you.
⚡
Pro Tip: Get an SSD If your laptop is more than 3 years old, changing your old hard drive to an SSD will make it 10x faster. We do this at our store!
