Keeping Your Laptop Fast & Healthy

Is your laptop running slow? Don't worry, you don't always need a new one! Here are 3 simple things you can do today:

🧹
1. Clean the Dust Dust blocked fans make your laptop overheat and slow down. A quick clean once a month works wonders!
🗑️
2. Clear Your Desktop Too many files on your desktop can slow down your startup. Move them to folders in 'Documents' instead.
🔌
3. Power Savvy Don't keep your charger plugged in after 100%. Unplug and let the battery work — it keeps it healthy longer.
